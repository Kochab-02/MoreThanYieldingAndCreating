# More Than Yielding And Creating
This is a Minecraft mod adding various interesting stuff, including single and multi-player options.

However, some features are not available in single-player mode.

Until now(2026.5.18,mod version 1.4.1-alpha,all the features included are listed below).

For Chinese information,please visit McMod and search for author.

中文信息详见mcmod百科，搜索作者“北极二”。

-Enchantments

1.Decisive Strike

  Max level 4, can be attached to swords and axes. 
  
  Gives a basic damage bonus of 0.5×(level+1) points.

  When attacking a mob whose health is less than 5%×(level+1), there's a possibility of 5%+level×2%   that the mob will get instant killed.

2.Back to Square One

  Max level 1, is curse, can be attached to weapons, tools and fishing rods.

  When attacking a mob whose health is less than 10%, there's a 50% chance that the mob regenerates to   max health.

  When digging blocks whose hardness is above 2, there's a 40% chance that the digging fails.

  When fishing, there's a 70% chance that nothing is fished.

  *Compensation:(can config)

  When negative techiques are triggered, player gets a compensation.

  Attacking:5s Power I ; Digging:5s Haste I ; Fishing:5s Luck I ;

3.Total Eradication

  Max level 5, can be attached to swords.

  Gives a basic damage bonus of 0.25×(level+2) points.

  After killing a monster, player get a 2% × level × maxHealth damage bonus when attacking next     monster.
4.Weakness Focus

  Max level 4, can be attached to swords.

  When attacking, level×(target armor)×2% damage given will be transferred to true damage, while    the remaining damage goes through normal calculation(armor & potion etc.)

5.Strike of Heaven

  Max level 4, can be attached to weapons(sword and axe). When attacking, player get a damage bonus depending on player's fall distance. Also, attacking will reset player's fall distance.

6.Remote Guard

  Max level 1, can be attached to shields.

  Allows player to do the blocking skill (see below) without being teleported.

-Effects

1.Annihilation of Humanity

  Is negative effect, when killing any friendly baby mobs, player gets this effect.

  Summons a demon (see below) when the effect ends.

  If player objectively removes the effect, it'll summon a demon, a pair of double demon (see below) and a corrupted demon.

  The demon always spawns at z+5, requires 3-block-high air.
